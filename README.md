# Churchill Rocket Range Transition Digital Archive

A curated digital collection documenting the transition of the Churchill Rocket Range from a military facility under the Canadian Armament Research and Development Establishment (CARDE) to a civilian space research facility operated by the National Research Council (NRC).

## Purpose

This archive serves as a structured repository of primary and secondary sources that highlight the historical, technological, and institutional changes during the 1960sâ€“1970s. It focuses on:

- The 1964 handover from military to civilian control
- Post-transition rocket testing and research activities
- Key personnel and scientific contributions during the NRC era

## Collection Scope

The archive includes digitized documents, photographs, technical schematics, and biographical materials that illuminate the shift from defense-oriented rocket development to peaceful scientific exploration.
